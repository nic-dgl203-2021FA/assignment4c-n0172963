The Whale Island

The Island Whale is my last critique drawing created in FIN-110 on Fall term 2020. The basic concept is about the greatness of nature and how human lives are bonded with it. The project was inspired by the Indigenous origin stories about a turtle island, which also stands for deeply-held respect for the environment.
